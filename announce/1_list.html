PGP Keysigning

If you intend to participate in the PGP keysigning event at FOSDEM 2011, you should have submitted your key to the keyserver before the deadline. The final list of participants is now available. If you are not on the list, you will not be able to participate. Check for announcements and submit your key in time for next year's even.

If you are participating in the keysigning event (ie: you have submitted your key to the keyserver), you should now download the final list of participants and follow its instructions closely.

The final list of participants is available from https://ksp.fosdem.org/files/.

If there is a trust-path between you and the author, you should verify the list's detached signature using:

% gpg --verify ksp-fosdem2011.txt.asc ksp-fosdem2011.txt

Please bring the printed list, a pen and appropriate form of identification with you to FOSDEM on Sunday. Be on time!
