PGP Keysigning

If you intend to participate in the PGP keysigning event at FOSDEM 2011, you must submit the keys you would like to have signed to the keyserver listening on ksp.fosdem.org. If you are using GnuPG, this can easily be accomplished with:

% gpg --keyserver ksp.fosdem.org --send-key [keyid]

You may want to verify that your submission made it to the keyserver by checking the list of submitted keys at http://ksp.fosdem.org/.

The deadline for submissions is Monday 31 January 2011. After this date, the keyserver will no longer accept submissions and the official keylist will be published. Further instructions on what to do with the keylist and how to participate in the keysigning party itself will be communicated at that time.

Submission / Web of Trust Graphs

During the submission period, graphs will be generated of the density of the web of trust and the rate at which keys are being submitted. You can find these graphs at: https://ksp.fosdem.org/graphs/
